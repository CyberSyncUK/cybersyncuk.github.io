My Story

Hello, world! Thanks for stopping by. I'm Josh, Tech Enthusiast, Cyber Security Advocate, Microsoft 365 expert and (I like to think) an all round decent guy.

My professional journey hasn't been a straight line, but it has always been about protection and precision. I began my career in the NHS, spending years working across Occupational Therapy and Physiotherapy before rounding out my time serving in the Ambulance Service as an IHCD frontline technician. While I thrived on the practical aspect of helping people, the relentless demands of a life working in the NHS led me to pivot for my family. To all my former NHS colleagues who are still out there smashing it every day, you guys are the best this country has to offer and we owe you big time!

I transitioned into Finance (strange given that I hate Excel), where I discovered a hidden passion for systems through large-scale ERP migrations like SAP and OpenText VIM. Realizing my future lay in technology, I took the leap into an Infrastructure Apprenticeship at the ripe old age of 28. I turned a late start into a fast track, earning a Distinction and rapidly progressing to Technical & Security Manager. I helped the business grow at scale and led the migration, configuration and deployment of new technologies including a new Remote Monitoring & Management tool, Professional Services Automation ticketing platform, M365 automation tools and my personal favorite, leading the charge on deploying the entire Huntress Cyber Security stack for our clients.
